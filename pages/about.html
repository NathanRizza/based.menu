About:

based.menu is a simple, lightweight website you can use to find recipes to cook based on the ingredients you provide. It uses based.cooking as the repository for recipes.

It's useful if your pantry is running low and you don't know what you can make with the ingredients you have on hand, or if you just want to cook with some specific ingredients. I would recommend having a have a text file that you paste into the search bar, consisting of ingredients you typically have in your pantry.
